# MyBusiness.gov summary

## Context

Small businesses often need to navigate permits, approvals, and compliance steps across multiple agencies. The journey is fragmented, hard to understand, and full of repeated effort.

## My contribution

- Analysed the service problem
- Identified the points of confusion in the current process
- Framed an AI-assisted concept that could guide users through the next step
- Presented the idea in a way that made sense to non-technical stakeholders

## Outcome

The project was the only nominated entry from Tasmania and was recognised with the Spirit of GovHack award in the region.
