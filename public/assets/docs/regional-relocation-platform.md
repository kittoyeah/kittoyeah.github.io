# Regional relocation platform

## Problem

People moving from metropolitan to regional areas often face scattered information, inconsistent guidance, and difficulty comparing places, services, and lifestyle needs.

## Approach

- Used research and journey mapping to frame the problem
- Converted messy user and data needs into a clearer product direction
- Prioritised usability and decision support over feature sprawl

## Result

The concept reached the top 50 in the IBM watsonx.ai Hackathon 2025 and stands as a good example of how I move from ambiguity to structured product thinking quickly.
