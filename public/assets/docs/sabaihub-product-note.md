# SabaiHub product note

## What it is

SabaiHub is a production SaaS platform built for Thai massage businesses in Australia. It focuses on operational workflows that are often split across generic booking software, spreadsheets, and manual coordination.

## What I owned

- Product framing and scope definition
- Requirements analysis
- Solution design
- Implementation and testing
- Deployment and iteration

## Why it matters

The product shows that I can move beyond analysis and into execution. It is not just a concept deck or prototype. It is a working system shaped around a real service business context.

## Technical stack

Next.js, React, TypeScript, Prisma, Supabase, PostgreSQL, Vercel
